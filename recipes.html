Scrambled Eggs

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 tbsp Milk
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions: Beat eggs, add milk and salt. Cook on medium heat while stirring gently.

Guacamole

Ingredients

  • 2 Avocados
  • 1/2 Lime, juiced
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions: Mash avocados, mix with lime juice and salt. Serve fresh.

Tomato Soup

Ingredients

  • 4 Tomatoes
  • 1 Onion
  • 2 cups Water

Instructions: Boil chopped vegetables and blend until smooth. Add salt and pepper to taste.

Fruit Smoothie

Ingredients

  • 1 Banana
  • 1 cup Strawberries
  • 1/2 cup Yogurt

Instructions: Blend all ingredients until smooth. Chill and serve.

Pasta Salad

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Cooked Pasta
  • 1/2 cup Cherry Tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup Feta Cheese

Instructions: Mix all ingredients with olive oil and herbs. Serve chilled.

Vegetable Stir Fry

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Broccoli
  • 1 Bell Pepper
  • 2 tbsp Soy Sauce

Instructions: Stir-fry veggies in oil, add soy sauce, and cook until tender.

Peanut Butter Toast

Ingredients

  • 2 slices Bread
  • 2 tbsp Peanut Butter

Instructions: Toast bread, spread peanut butter, and enjoy!

Ingredients
